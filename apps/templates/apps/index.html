Applications

There are no applications yet, only sample visualisation.

That's because the point of opening our data is to let others take it and build something useful, that we wouldn't have thought of.

What we can do is make it available in useful ways, so that external services are easier to build. Right now, the data is available in raw form, but with your help, we can improve this platform to deliver the data more usefully.

That said, we have a few visualizations to show off the potential of this dataset

Sample Visualizations

Caveat: these graphs are at most as accurate and complete as the dataset they are built on.

Number of cases per month

This is a very simple barchart showing the number of cases processed by month between 2002 and 2012. The line shows the average yearly totals.

Map

The map below shows the average age of offenders at each court on the map, over the 10-year period. Black is youngest (25 years old average), white is older (36 years old average). Leave the pointer on a circle to see the actual value.

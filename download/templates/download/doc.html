The court proceedings dataset

This page describes the Court Proceedings Datasets. The set consists of 10 CSV files, with each line providing information about a magistrates court proceeding, or a crown court trial. Only cases with adult offenders are included.

Caveats

MOJ provides no guarantee as to the accuracy and the completeness of the data provided.

License and Copyright

The data is distributed under Crown Copyright and licensed under the Open Government Licence.

Contents of the Zip file

  • Green Book (General codes) 2014.xls
  • Orange Compendium (A to Z Acts and sections codes) 2014.xls
  • hack02.csv
  • hack03.csv
  • ...csv

The CSV files are the data itself, described below, and the XLS provide table for decoding the indexes in the file.
